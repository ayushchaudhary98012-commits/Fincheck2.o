Why verify your trust score?

Borrowers want to work with legitimate, verified lenders. A higher trust score indicates complete identity and funds verification, reducing matching frictions.

  • Bronze (30+): Basic account verification.
  • Silver (50+): Identity & address verified. High compatibility matches.
  • Gold (70+): Verified source of funds. Higher acceptance rates.
  • Platinum (85+): Institutional/Corporate verified grade. Instant trust.
